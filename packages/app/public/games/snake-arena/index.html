About Snake Arena

Snake Arena is an exciting multiplayer snake game where you compete against other players in real-time. Grow your snake by eating food and avoid colliding with other snakes or the walls. The longer you survive, the higher your score!

How to Play

  • Use arrow keys or WASD to move
  • Eat food to grow your snake
  • Avoid hitting walls and other snakes
  • Compete for the highest score
